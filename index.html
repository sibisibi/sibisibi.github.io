Leptomeningeal Metastases at Recurrence in IDH-wildtype Glioblastomas: Incidence, Risk Factors, and Prognosis Based on Postcontrast FLAIR Imaging
Yaewon Park, Geon Jang, Sibeen Kim, Kyunghwa Han, Nayoung Shin, Sungsoo Ahn, Jonghee Chang, Sehoon Kim, Rajan Jain, Seungkoo Lee
In Progress

We identified a 10.8% incidence of leptomeningeal metastases (LM) at recurrence in IDH-wildtype glioblastoma patients, with younger age and ventricular entry during surgery as significant predictors; however, the presence of LM did not markedly affect overall survival, suggesting that the risk of LM should not deter optimal surgical intervention.

MRI radiomics may predict early tumor recurrence in patients with sinonasal squamous cell carcinoma
Chaejung Park*, Seohee Choi*, Dain Kim, Sibeen Kim, Kyunghwa Han, Sungsoo Ahn, Wonhee Lee, Eunchang Choi, Kichang Keum*, Jinna Kim*
European Radiology, Nov 2023

We leverage MRI radiomics and machine learning to revolutionize the early detection of local failure in sinonasal squamous cell carcinoma, significantly enhancing prognosis accuracy and patient treatment outcomes.
